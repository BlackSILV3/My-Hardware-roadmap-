Industry Reality

Semiconductor Foundry Costs

Understanding the economics of chip fabrication is essential for realistic career and business planning. Your starting point is the free Google OpenMPW shuttle program.

Your path: Start with free OpenMPW tape-outs using open-source tools (OpenROAD, Magic VLSI, Skywater 130nm PDK). Build a portfolio of working chip designs. Target fabless company roles at Qualcomm, NVIDIA, Intel. A physical foundry requires billions in capital -- but a fabless chip design company requires skills, a laptop, and an efabless account.
